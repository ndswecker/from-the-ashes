## ‚ò†Ô∏è Corruption of Dominion

Every dawn, the wearer makes a **DC 15 Wisdom saving throw**.

### **On a Success**
- No corruption is gained.  
- The bracers whisper faintly but exert no further control.

### **On a Failure**
Roll the **Corruption Die (1d4)**:

1. **Dominance Hunger**  
   Intrusive thoughts urge you to control others.  
   Advantage on Persuasion checks against free creatures.

2. **Rigid Obedience**  
   You exhibit Silver Veil authoritarian tendencies.  
   In your next combat, you must spend one action issuing a command (verbal, narrative, or tactical).

3. **Banite Influence**  
   You distrust challenges to your authority.  
   Disadvantage on Insight checks until your next long rest.

4. **Loss of Self**  
   Gain 1 **Corruption Point**.  
   At **3 Corruption Points**, you become **Cursed by Dominion**.

### ‚ö† Forced Attunement
If you **fail this corruption check while not attuned**, you immediately become *attuned* to the bracers.  
This cannot be resisted unless you are unconscious.

## üîí Curse of Dominion (At 3 Corruption Points)

When you reach **3 Corruption Points**, Bane brands your spirit with an invisible mark of dominance.

You become **Cursed**, and the following effects apply:

- The bracers **fuse to your skin** and cannot be removed or unattuned by any voluntary means.  
- You cannot willingly assist in their removal.  
- You cannot willingly reduce yourself to 0 HP.  
- You instinctively resist attempts to strip you of authority or status.  
- The bracers exert a constant mental pressure encouraging domination and control.

### **Only Method of Removal**

The curse is broken **only if another creature reduces you to 0 HP**.

- The damage must come from an **external source** (attack, spell, trap, environmental hazard).  
- You cannot willingly cooperate in this process.  
- When you hit 0 HP, the bracers **fall away**, inert.  
- They must be removed **before you are healed** or they will immediately reattach.

Once removed, the character is no longer cursed and their corruption points reset to **0**.

The bracers remain dangerous and corruptive to anyone who touches them thereafter.
