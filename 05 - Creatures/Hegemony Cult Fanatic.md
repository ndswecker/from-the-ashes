# Hegemony Cult Fanatic

*Medium humanoid (human), lawful evil*

---

## Description

Hegemony Cult Fanatics are ordained agitators and battlefield preachers of Jehovah’s Hegemony.  
They do not merely fight—they **shape fear**, weaponize scripture, and direct lesser believers to die correctly.

They believe panic is proof of righteousness.

---

## Stat Block

**Armor Class:** 13 (leather armor, shield of faith when active)  
**Hit Points:** 33 (6d8 + 6)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

| STR | DEX | CON | INT | WIS | CHA |
|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|-----|
| 11 (+0) | 14 (+2) | 12 (+1) | 10 (+0) | 13 (+1) | 14 (+2) |

**Saving Throws:** Wis +3, Cha +4  
**Skills:** Intimidation +4, Religion +2  
**Senses:** Passive Perception 11  
**Languages:** Common  
**Challenge:** 2 (450 XP)

---

## Traits

**Fanatical Devotion.**  
The fanatic has advantage on saving throws against being frightened or charmed.

**Martyr’s Conviction.**  
When the fanatic is reduced below half its hit points, it gains advantage on its next spell attack or spell save DC increases by +1 until the end of its next turn.

---

## Spellcasting

The Hegemony Cult Fanatic is a 4th-level spellcaster.  
Its spellcasting ability is **Wisdom** (spell save DC 13, +5 to hit with spell attacks).

### At Will
- *Thaumaturgy* (scripture shouting, doors slamming, eyes glowing)

### 1st Level (4 slots)
- *Command*
- *Shield of Faith*
- *Sanctuary*

### 2nd Level (3 slots)
- *Hold Person*
- *Bane*
- *Inflict Wounds*

---

## Actions

### Mace  
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +4 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
*Hit:* 5 (1d6 + 2) bludgeoning damage.

### Inflict Wounds (Spell)  
*Melee Spell Attack:* +5 to hit, reach 5 ft., one creature.  
*Hit:* 16 (3d10) necrotic damage.

---

## Bonus Actions

### Invoke Doctrine (1/Encounter)  
The fanatic chooses one allied creature within 30 ft.  
That creature gains advantage on its next attack roll and advantage on its next saving throw before the end of its next turn.

---

## Tactics

- **Opening Round:** *Hold Person* or *Command* to disrupt defenders  
- **Mid-Fight:** *Bane* or *Sanctuary* to apply pressure and protect allies  
- **Finisher:** *Inflict Wounds* when an opening appears  
- Never retreats during Phase I encounters  
- Shouts scripture constantly to heighten panic

---

## Role in Encounters

- Primary control and panic engine  
- Shapes the battlefield rather than dealing sustained damage  
- Designed to threaten NPCs without executing them outright  
- Death should feel like the collapse of coordination, not relief
