# Yellen of Fox Island (Level 4)
*Medium humanoid (human), chaotic good*

**Class:** Rogue (Lvl 4)  
**Background:** Criminal (Smuggler & Island Guide)  
**Occupation:** Highway Robber turned Scout  
**Armor Class:** 16 (studded leather)  
**Hit Points:** 23 (4d8)  
**Speed:** 30 ft.

**STR** 9 (−1)  
**DEX** 18 (+4)  
**CON** 11 (+0)  
**INT** 14 (+2)  
**WIS** 13 (+1)  
**CHA** 15 (+2)

**Saving Throws:** Dexterity +6, Intelligence +4  
**Skills:** Acrobatics +6, Deception +4, Intimidation +4, Perception +3, Sleight of Hand +8, Stealth +8  
**Tools:** Gaming sets (playing cards), thieves' tools  
**Senses:** passive Perception 13  
**Languages:** Common, Dwarvish  
**Challenge:** 1 (200 XP)

---

## **Rogue Features**

### **Sneak Attack (1/turn)**
Yellen deals an extra **2d6** damage when she hits a creature with advantage, or when an ally is within 5 feet of the target and Yellen doesn’t have disadvantage.

### **Criminal Contact**
Yellen keeps a network of smugglers and ferrymen stretching from Fox Island to Puget Sound. She can get messages to contacts reliably, even over long distances.

### **Cunning Action**
Bonus action each turn to Dash, Disengage, Hide, or Use an Object.  
She may also use Sleight of Hand or thieves’ tools as a bonus action.

### **Expertise**
Yellen doubles her proficiency bonus for:
- Sleight of Hand  
- Stealth

### **Second-Story Work**
Climbing doesn’t cost extra movement. Running jump distance increases by 4 feet.

---

## **Actions**

### **Two-Weapon Fighting**
When Yellen attacks with a shortsword, she can make a second shortsword attack as a **bonus action**.  
- Off-hand shortsword: **3 (1d6) piercing** damage.

### **Shortsword**
*Melee Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, reach 5 ft., one target.  
**Hit:** 7 (1d6 + 4) piercing damage.

### **Light Crossbow**
*Ranged Weapon Attack:* +6 to hit, range 80/320 ft., one target.  
**Hit:** 8 (1d8 + 4) piercing damage.
